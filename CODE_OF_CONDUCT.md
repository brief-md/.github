# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be respectful. Assume good intent. Focus on the work.

---

## The Longer Version

BRIEF.md is an open project and we want it to stay welcoming to contributors from any background, domain, or level of experience.

**We expect everyone to:**
- Be respectful in discussions, issues, and pull requests
- Give constructive feedback and receive it graciously
- Assume good intent unless clearly demonstrated otherwise
- Stay focused on the work, not the person

**We won't tolerate:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissive or contemptuous behaviour toward contributors
- Sustained disruption of discussions

---

## Reporting

If something happens that makes you uncomfortable, email [hello.briefmd@gmail.com](mailto:hello.briefmd@gmail.com). Reports will be handled with discretion.

---

This code of conduct applies across all BRIEF.md spaces — GitHub repos, issues, pull requests, and any other community spaces.
